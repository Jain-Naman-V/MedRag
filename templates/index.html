About MedRAG RAG

MedRAG RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation) is a powerful tool designed to help healthcare professionals extract and analyze information from patient medical records and documents.

Our system uses advanced natural language processing to:

  • Extract key medical information from uploaded documents
  • Answer specific questions about patient history
  • Summarize important findings and medical data
  • Provide quick access to relevant information across multiple documents
